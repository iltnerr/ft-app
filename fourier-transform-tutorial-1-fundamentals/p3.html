Determining the Fourier Transform of Different Even Functions.

💡
The last page of this tutorial applies the ideas you have learned so far to other even functions. That is, the triangular, sinc and gaussian function.

Interesting aspects to consider
  • The Fourier transform of a rectangular function is a sinc function.
  • The Fourier transform of a sinc function is a rectangular function.
  • The Fourier transform of a triangular function is a squared sinc function.
  • The Fourier transform of a gaussian function is a gaussian function.
Also, remember that the Fourier transforms depicted here have no imaginary component, since we are still dealing with even functions.
